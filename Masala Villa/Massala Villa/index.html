Welcome Massala Villa

At Massala Villa Indian Restaurant, We endeavour to create mouthwatering and delicious Indian food to tingle your tastebuds. Our ultimate aim is to satisfy our customers with the finest Indian cuisine along with exceptional customer service. Our secret lies in the scrupulous mix and balance of the spices and herbs we use to create our food. We source our ingredients locally where possible & our authentic spices imported from the Indian sub continent. Our curteous staff, high standard of food and atmosphere of our restaurant is what makes Massala Villa successful. Call us & book your table, we're sure you won't be disappointed. Thanks , Massala Villa management.
